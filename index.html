  1. AI Engineer – Healthcare, Finance, Retail, Agriculture, Education, Manufacturing

    Remuneration (India)

    ₹8-30+ Lakhs per annum

    Skills

    Python, ML/DL frameworks, domain-specific knowledge (e.g., finance, healthcare), data visualization, API integration.

    Entry-Level

    Mid-Career

    Senior/Leadership

    Advance to Principal AI Engineer or Head of AI for a Business Unit. You'll set the technical strategy for an entire department, mentor teams, and become a subject matter expert, identifying high-impact AI opportunities within the domain.
