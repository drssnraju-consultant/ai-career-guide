1. AI for Domain Applications

  • AI Engineer – Healthcare, Finance, Retail, Agriculture, Education, Manufacturing

    Remuneration (India)

    ₹8-30+ Lakhs per annum

    Skills

    Python, ML/DL frameworks, domain-specific knowledge (e.g., finance, healthcare), data visualization, API integration.

    Entry-Level

    Start as a Junior AI Engineer or Data Analyst within a specific sector. Focus on learning the industry's data types, regulations, and core problems. Key skills include Python, foundational ML, and domain-specific tools.

    Mid-Career

    Progress to an AI Specialist or Lead Engineer. You'll design and implement solutions for more complex, domain-specific challenges, such as fraud detection or patient outcome prediction. This stage requires deep industry knowledge and the ability to lead small projects.

    Senior/Leadership

    Advance to Principal AI Engineer or Head of AI for a Business Unit. You'll set the technical strategy for an entire department, mentor teams, and become a subject matter expert, identifying high-impact AI opportunities within the domain.

5. Entry / Early-Career Roles

  • AI Intern – Research Intern

    Remuneration (India)

    ₹20,000-50,000 per month (Stipend)

    Skills

    Python, basic ML concepts, data handling, academic research skills.

    Entry-Level

    This is the starting point. Focus on learning fundamental concepts, contributing to small projects, and building your professional network.

    Mid-Career

    An intern's career path is a direct transition into a full-time role, such as a Junior Data Scientist or AI Engineer, by demonstrating strong project work and a willingness to learn.

    Senior/Leadership

    Success at the intern level is measured by securing a full-time offer, not by moving into a senior role directly. Seniority is achieved after years of experience as a full-time employee.

  • AI Support Engineer – Technical Support (AI products)

    Remuneration (India)

    ₹3-8 Lakhs per annum

    Skills

    Problem-solving, communication, basic AI knowledge, technical troubleshooting.

    Entry-Level

    Begin by providing support to customers using AI products. Your focus is on troubleshooting issues and understanding common user problems.

    Mid-Career

    Progress to a Senior Support Engineer. You'll handle more complex technical issues and provide feedback to the engineering team to improve the product.

    Senior/Leadership

    Advance to a Manager of Technical Support. You'll lead a team of support engineers and set the strategy for providing excellent customer support for AI products.

  • AI Teaching Assistant – Lab Assistant

    Remuneration (India)

    ₹20,000-40,000 per month (Stipend)

    Skills

    Strong foundational knowledge of AI/ML, communication, mentorship.

    Mid-Career

    You can transition into an applied research role or a junior position in a specialized AI field after demonstrating expertise and project work.

    Senior/Leadership

    Seniority is not typical for this role. It is a transitional role that prepares you for more senior positions in either academia (e.g., as a researcher) or industry.
